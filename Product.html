What is Phytos?

Phytos is proudly made in the Philippines, from planting to manufacturing. The key ingredient, Sesbania Grandiflora (locally known as "Katuray"), is grown, harvested, and processed entirely within the Philippines, ensuring the highest level of quality and supporting local agriculture.


Phytos is rich in phytosterols and flavonoids, which are considered health-promoting and disease-preventing dietary supplements, especially for chronic conditions associated with inflammation such as cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, and certain cancers. The compound sativan is one of the flavonoids found in Sesbania grandiflora.

Sesbania grandiflora is widely studied for its medicinal properties.

Sesbania grandiflora (Agathi) leaves contain plant constituents that help provide four (4) ways of natural protection:

  • (i) Contains essential vitamins, minerals, and amino acids;
  • (ii) Contains phytochemicals known to protect the body's cells from harmful
    elements that could damage them and potentially cause cancer;
  • (iii) It is a recognized antioxidant;
  • (iv) It has demonstrated antibacterial properties.

How Can Phytos Help Manage Diabetes?

Phytos supports individuals managing diabetes through the natural compounds found in Agathi leaves. While not a cure, Phytos offers essential nutrients that may aid in maintaining healthy blood sugar levels and improving overall well-being.


While Phytos is not intended to replace medical treatments, it is a natural, plant-based supplement that may complement a balanced diet and healthy lifestyle in managing diabetes symptoms. Always consult your healthcare professional before adding Phytos to your routine.
