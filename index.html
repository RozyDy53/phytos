WHAT IS PHYTOS?

PHYTOS is proudly made in the Philippines, from planting to manufacturing.

The key ingredient, Sesbania grandiflora (locally known as "Katuray"), is grown, harvested, and processed entirely within the Philippines, ensuring the highest level of quality while supporting local agriculture.

“Katuray” in PHYTOS contains phytosterols and flavonoids, which are considered to have health benefits as a dietary supplement (Thissera et al., 2020).
